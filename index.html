A Brief Overview of Lesbians in Media

As long as media has existed, in its many forms, lesbians and sapphics have been a part of it. In ancient Greece, we had Sappho (the namesake of sapphics) writing poems, her most famous being those that were about other women. And, of course, lesbians continue to be a vital, if underserved part of media to this day.

Lesbians create music, write literature and are involved in all steps of TV shows and movies, from writing them and starring in them to directing them and hair and make-up. Though it is more rare for lesbian stories to be told, due to not only sexism, but also homophobia and the wage-gap.
